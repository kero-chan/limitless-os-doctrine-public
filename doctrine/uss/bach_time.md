## The principle

Bach is the **only** person who can wire our engine into a running game. That is irreplaceable. Everything else — schemas, math, art generation, documentation, status updates, asset pipeline — can be AI'd or AI-assisted. So we **protect Bach's hours for what only he can do**, and we use AI for everything else. That is how the team "brings value" — not by Bach burning himself out doing AI-replaceable work, but by Bach doing the 3 things no AI can do.

---

## Bach's 3 Protected Tasks (this sprint)

1. **Wire USS into the live frontend.** The Universal Slot Solver runs as a spec today. Bach turns it into a running game.
2. **Confirm + implement one-click art-stock re-skin.** Same game, swap config, new art. This is the "many games, many slots, automated" promise made real.
3. **Implement cascade + anticipation timing hooks in the renderer.** The dopamine layer Chris keeps asking for.

**If Bach is doing anything outside these 3 this sprint, we are wasting him.**
